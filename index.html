About Me

I am a professional translator and consultant with extensive experience working across English, Tajik, Russian, and Persian (Farsi). My expertise spans various fields including legal, business, technical, and general translation.

Based in Tajikistan, I offer not just linguistic services but also deep regional knowledge that helps international clients navigate the local business and cultural landscape. My work is characterized by meticulous attention to detail, cultural sensitivity, and a commitment to delivering accurate, context-appropriate translations.

With a background that includes working with international organizations, businesses, and government entities, I understand the importance of precision in communication and the nuances that can make or break a project in this region.
